## What is Mutual Divorce?

Mutual divorce is a legal process where both husband and wife jointly agree to dissolve their marriage. It involves:
- Filing a joint petition
- Agreeing on alimony/maintenance
- Settling child custody (if applicable)
- Completing court formalities

**Advantages:**
- No long disputes
- Faster resolution
- Less emotional and financial stress

## Who Can Apply for Mutual Divorce?

You are eligible if:
- You have been married for at least 1 year
- Both parties agree to divorce
- You are living separately
- Issues like alimony and custody are settled

## Common Questions

**Can mutual divorce be completed quickly?**  
Yes, with proper documentation and agreement, delays can be minimized.

**Is court appearance required?**  
Yes, both parties must appear during hearings.

**Can one party withdraw later?**  
Yes, consent can be withdrawn before final decree.
