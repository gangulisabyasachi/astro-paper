## The Harmeeta Singh Precedent: Stopping Foreign Oppression

In the famous case of *Harmeeta Singh v. Rajat Taneja*, the Indian High Court’s response was a game-changer for any NRI divorce lawyer in Kolkata to cite:

- **The Anti-Suit Injunction**: The court restrained the husband from continuing proceedings in a US court.
- **Jurisdictional Supremacy**: It ruled that an Indian citizen cannot be forced to litigate in an "inconvenient forum" (*forum non-conveniens*) just because the other spouse moved there.
- **Mandatory Compliance**: The husband was forced to halt the foreign divorce and respect the Indian court's jurisdiction.

### 1. Anti-Suit Injunctions in the Kolkata High Court
If your spouse has initiated a case in the UK, USA, or Canada, we can move the **Kolkata High Court** to issue an injunction. This prevents them from obtaining an ex-parte decree that could jeopardize your alimony and child custody rights in India.

### 2. Maintenance under BNSS 2023
With the implementation of the **Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) 2023**, the process for maintenance has become more stringent and time-bound. We ensure foreign income is accurately disclosed to secure a fair maintenance order.

### 3. Voiding Foreign Decrees
Under Section 13 of the CPC, foreign decrees are often non-binding in India if they were obtained through fraud or by ignoring Indian personal laws. We help you challenge and void such decrees.
